This page is dedicated the the Kenneth V. McKenzie 's equation for calculating the celerity of the sound in seawater. The celerity depend in the temperature, the salinity and the depth where the sound is moving.

celerity(D,S,T) Meter by second = 1448.96 + 4.591T - 5.304 x 10-2T2 + 2.374 x 10-4T3 + 1.340 (S-35) + 1.630 x 10-2D + 1.675 x 10-7D2 - 1.025 x 10-2T(S - 35) - 7.139 x 10-13TD3
